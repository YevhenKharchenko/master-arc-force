Game Description

Tower of God: NEW WORLD is an epic RPG experience based on the smash-hit webtoon with over 6 billion views worldwide. Step into a visually stunning world of mystery, power, and ambition as you climb the fabled Tower.

Experience deep lore, dynamic gameplay, and massive rewards in a game that brings the spirit of the Tower of God to life.

Join Bam, Khun, Rak, and many other familiar faces in a brand-new story exclusive to the game. Recruit heroes, unleash their ultimate skills, and engage in thrilling battles while strategizing with unique Elements and Positions.
