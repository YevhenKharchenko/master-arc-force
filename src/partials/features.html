Game Features

Character Recruitment:

Collect and upgrade a wide roster of Tower dwellers.

Strategic Combat:

Engage in real-time battles using elemental affinities and team roles.

Ultimate Skills:

Unleash special moves unique to each character.

Shared Progression:

Use one set of upgrades across multiple characters.

Loot System:

Earn resources even while offline.

PvE & PvP Modes:

Battle through story-driven levels and challenge other players.

Event Challenges:

Take part in seasonal content and earn exclusive rewards.
