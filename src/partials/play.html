How to Play

Start Your Journey:

Begin the game as Bam and unlock new heroes.

Build a Team:

Recruit and upgrade your favorite characters.

Unleash Skills:

Tap into unique ultimate attacks during battles.

Battle with Strategy:

Use Elements and Positions for tactical advantages.

Earn Loot Automatically:

Collect upgrade materials with passive systems.

Join Events:

Participate in challenges and limited-time story arcs.

Climb the Tower:

Progress through stages and uncover new secrets.
