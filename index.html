Game Description

Tower of God: NEW WORLD is an epic RPG experience based on the smash-hit webtoon with over 6 billion views worldwide. Step into a visually stunning world of mystery, power, and ambition as you climb the fabled Tower.

Experience deep lore, dynamic gameplay, and massive rewards in a game that brings the spirit of the Tower of God to life.

Join Bam, Khun, Rak, and many other familiar faces in a brand-new story exclusive to the game. Recruit heroes, unleash their ultimate skills, and engage in thrilling battles while strategizing with unique Elements and Positions.

Main Characters

  • Twenty-Fifth Bam

    The mysterious boy who enters the Tower seeking his only friend, Rachel. Kind-hearted but powerful beyond expectations.

  • Khun Aguero Agnes

    A strategic genius from a prestigious family. Cold and calculating, but deeply loyal to his companions.

  • Rak Wraithraiser

    A proud and comedic spear bearer with a strong sense of honor. He calls everyone a "turtle."

  • Rachel

    Bam’s enigmatic friend whose motivations unfold layer by layer. Her role in the Tower is complex and controversial.

  • Endorsi Jahad

    A fierce and confident Princess of Jahad who masks vulnerability with strength and arrogance.

  • Hatz & Shibisu

    Memorable side characters with sharp skills and strong development throughout the journey.
